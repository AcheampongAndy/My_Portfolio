About Me

Andrews Acheampong is a Teaching and Research Assistant at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics. His research interests lie in integrating genomics, transcriptomics, and statistical genetics to develop predictive models for complex diseases through multi-omics data analysis.
